## Editor's Note

_The most important development this week is the U.S. directive for diplomats to push back on foreign data-sovereignty rules, because cross-border data controls now shape enterprise AI architecture as much as model quality does. For CTOs and policy leads, this is a near-term planning issue: either keep a centralized stack viable, or fund regionalized data, model, and compliance footprints sooner than expected._
