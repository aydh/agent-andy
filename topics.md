## Noise Filters

Stories matching these patterns will be deprioritised or skipped unless
genuinely significant:

- Funding announcements without product/technical substance
- "X partners with Y" press releases with no shipping date
- Opinion pieces rehashing existing debates
- Benchmark claims without reproducible methodology
